Seeking a New Life

So I’m trying to find a way to get out of my parents house as soon as I graduate. I don’t have a plan, I’m thinking maybe college, but I don’t know where. And I don’t know what I would even study. My parents are crazy, they want me to go and do things but they don’t want me to leave the house. I can’t make them happy so I’m just going to stop trying.

A Place of My Own

SI just moved into my first apartment. I moved everything out of my parents house and into the new space. I have a roommate, but he doesn’t really like to talk much. As much as I needed to be living away from my parents, I don’t feel at home here. The place feels too empty, and too quiet. My parent’s house doesn’t feel like home anymore either without my things there. My mom has already started to put her office in my old room. I know it had to be done, I just hope the new place starts to feel like home soon.

Making these Games

A few people have asked me why I make these games, and I figured I would just put that answer here so I don’t have to repeat it. I started making games because I liked games. I decided to keep making games because it was one of the only enjoyable projects I’ve ever done. I’m glad my games have a larger audience now, but I also feel kind of pressured to keep up. I don’t know if I can be as good as everyone hopes, but I’m making an effort.
